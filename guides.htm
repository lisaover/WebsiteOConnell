Book Discussion

I love reading, and I love talking about the books I read. Sometimes, though, I have trouble getting past “I really, really loved it!” or “Ugh! I couldn’t stand it!” So I appreciate having some thoughtful questions to start the discussion.

My friend Lisa Dennis came up with insightful and stimulating questions about Penina Levine Is a Potato Pancake and Penina Levine Is a Hard-Boiled Egg. Lisa is the Coordinator of Children’s Collections at Carnegie Library of Pittsburgh.
